Park

Parks are like an oasis where people get away from the hustle and bustle of city life. They are where people go to have lunch, to take some exercise, or to forget about work for a while. Parks and recreation facilities are the places that people go to get healthy and stay fit. They also provide environmental benefits, including preserving plants and trees, decreasing air pollution and control temperatures and humidity.

Parks provide gathering places for families and social groups, as well as for individuals of all ages. Parks provide a sense of public pride and cohesion to every community.
